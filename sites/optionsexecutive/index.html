Options Strategy Finder

Ask a simple question, get actionable trade recommendations backed by real-time market data.

🎯 What It Does

The Options Strategy Finder takes your investment thesis and finds the best options trades to match it. Instead of manually searching through thousands of options contracts, you describe what you expect to happen and the system does the work.

Think of it as having a research analyst who instantly scans the entire options market to find trades that match your outlook.

⚡ How It Works

You Say
"I think Apple will reach $220 in the next 3 months. I want to risk no more than $2,000."
  1. Understands your goal — The system parses your target price, timeline, and risk tolerance
  2. Gathers market data — Pulls real-time options prices, volatility metrics, and market signals from your existing brokerage
  3. Evaluates strategies — Tests hundreds of possible trades against your criteria
  4. Ranks by fit — Scores each strategy based on probability of success, risk/reward, and trade quality
  5. Presents recommendations — Returns the top strategies with clear profit/loss scenarios

🎛 What You Control

Every search can be customized to match your investment style:

  • Target price — Where you think the stock is heading
  • Timeline — When you expect the move to happen
  • Maximum capital — How much you're willing to invest
  • Maximum risk — The most you're willing to lose
  • Strategy types — Limit to specific approaches (spreads only, covered calls, etc.)
  • Quality filters — Ensure trades have good liquidity and reasonable pricing
